Nokia

I worked at Nokia for just under a year, leading up to the their acquisition by Microsoft, as a Technical Accounts Tester.

I worked with carriers like T-Mobile and AT&T to make sure Nokia’s devices met all of their requirements and passed through their testing processes successfully. I helped external and internal testers run tests, reported and managed issues, and negotiated issue severity, priority, and fixes.

During my time there, I worked on several Lumia handsets, including:

  • The Lumia 1020
  • The Lumia 925
  • The Lumia 920
  • The Lumia 820/810
  • The Lumia 520
