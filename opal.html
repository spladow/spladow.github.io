Opal

Opal was a secure messaging and file sharing startup based in Los Angeles, CA. During our private alpha, which ran from August 2013 - January 2014, Opal was acquired by Google.

I joined Opal at the time of founding as the first non-founding member.

I did competitive and user research, produced low fidelity mockups, did some design work on the landing page and blog, as well as a very limited amount of web development work. I also wrote most of the marketing copy and edited the team’s blog posts.
